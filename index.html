Learning Phases

Phase 1: Foundational Mathematics

Build a robust mathematical toolkit with Linear Algebra, Calculus, and Probability & Statistics. Learn to implement concepts like Gaussian elimination and gradient descent in Python.

Resources: Strang’s “Introduction to Linear Algebra,” Khan Academy, Coursera’s Mathematics for ML.

Phase 2: Core Finance & AI Foundations

Dive into financial markets, derivatives, and Python-based financial modeling. Begin exploring machine learning with probability and statistics tailored for AI.

Resources: Fabozzi’s “Foundations of Financial Markets,” Hull’s “Options, Futures,” Hilpisch’s “Python for Finance.”

Phase 3: Advanced AI & Financial Integration

Master supervised/unsupervised learning, neural networks, and apply them to financial problems like algorithmic trading and risk management.

Resources: Ng’s “Deep Learning Specialization,” Lopez de Prado’s “Advances in Financial Machine Learning.”
